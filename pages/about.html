About Me

Most instructional designers build courses. I build learning systems.

Over 15 years, I've worked with global organizations — including Siemens — to design and engineer eLearning experiences that go beyond templates and SCORM checkboxes. My work combines deep instructional design expertise with technical skills most designers don't have: JavaScript, API integrations, AI tools, automation workflows, and LMS architecture.

I'm drawn to the intersection of design and engineering — the place where a well-structured learning experience meets a cleanly architected technical solution. I think in systems and platforms, not just slides.

I'm at my best when working with organizations that are serious about learning effectiveness — SaaS companies, EdTech platforms, and corporate L&D teams that want to move beyond content production into genuine learning innovation.

My Approach

I start every project by asking why. Why this format? Why this interaction? Why is a human doing this when a script could? That instinct has driven me to build AI-powered personalization systems, automated localization workflows, and scalable template architectures — all inside an L&D context where most practitioners accept the status quo.

I'm never satisfied with "good enough" when "genuinely better" is possible with a bit more technical thinking.
